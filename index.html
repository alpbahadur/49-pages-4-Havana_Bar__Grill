Welcome to Havana

Step into a world where the vibrant spirit of Cuba meets the tranquil beauty of coastal dining. At Havana Bar & Grill, we bring you an authentic taste of Cuban cuisine paired with stunning ocean views that will transport you to the shores of the Caribbean.

Our passionate chefs craft every dish with traditional Cuban recipes passed down through generations, using only the freshest ingredients and bold, authentic flavors. Whether you're here for our signature seafood dishes or our handcrafted cocktails, every moment at Havana is a celebration.
